The abode of mysticism, spirituality and natural beauty, Rishikesh is among the holiest places in India. Cradled in the lap of the Himalayas and adorned by the grace of the holy Ganges, this holy city is the confluence point of several mythological tales, faiths, and beliefs. A kaleidoscopic destination in the picturesque state of Uttarakhand, Rishikesh is known for its sanctity, bountiful nature, pleasant and calm ambience. It has been an abode of ascetics since, well, time immemorial. It is also famous as the gateway to the Garhwal mountains and sacred spots in them like the source of the Ganga, the Badrinath and Kedarnath shrines and more. Blessed with the beautiful, thundering river of Ganga, the great trek friendly rugged terrains, challenging peaks, difficult rapids and dense forests, Rishikesh makes for one of an ideal weekend getaway near Delhi. Rishikesh warmly welcomes thrill seekers, who can try out some activities such as white water rafting, bungee jumping, kayaking, zip lining, mountain biking and rappelling. The city also annually hosts the International Yoga Festival where yoga gurus, aficionados, and devotees from around the globe throng the place. Rishikesh, literally means Lord of the Senses which stands for Lord Vishnu and the city is blessed with the flowing of Holy Ganges. Ever since the Beatles rocked up at the ashram of the Maharishi Mahesh Yogi in the late '60s, Rishikesh has been a magnet for spiritual seekers. Cosseted by lush and dense thickets, guarded by fascinating peaks and adorned with alluring rivers, camping in Rishikesh becomes an exciting affair for the fun and thrill seekers! If you are among one of the adrenaline junkies, this is the place for you to explore, witness and discover the charm and appeal of the Mother Nature in its most pristine form.
